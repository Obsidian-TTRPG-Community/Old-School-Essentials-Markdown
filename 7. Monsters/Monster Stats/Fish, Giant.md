| Alignment        | Neutral                  |

- **Camouflage:** 70% chance of being mistaken for a rock or coral formation.
- **Grasping:** If mistaken for a rock and grasped, all 4 spines automatically hit.
- **Poison:** Causes death (**save vs poison**).
